Frequently Asked Questions

What does a typical cheese board include?

Our typical cheese board includes a variety of premium cheeses, salami, fresh fruit, nuts, crackers or grissini, chocolate and honey or jam. Each board is designed to offer a balanced and delicious tasting experience.

Do you offer customization options for the tables?s

Yes, we offer customization options to accommodate your preferences and dietary restrictions. You can choose specific cheeses, meats, and other accompaniments to create a board that suits your taste.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability and allow us to prepare your board to the highest standards. For larger events or catering orders, please contact us as soon as possible.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within a specific radius of our location. Delivery charges may apply depending on distance. Please contact us for more details on delivery options and rates.

Can I order tables for corporate events or large meetings?

Absolutely! We provide catering services for corporate events, parties and large meetings. Our team will work with you to create a custom menu that fits the needs of your event and ensures your guests' satisfaction.
